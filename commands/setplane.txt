#type: hscript

= setplane =

"""Sets the current plane."""

:usage: `setplane [-i <<array_index>>] <<plane_name>> `

NOTE:
    This command is only available in MPlay.



@options
    `-i <<array_index>> `:
        The index of the plane, for plane arrays (not normally used).

@related
    - [setcomp|Cmd:setcomp]
