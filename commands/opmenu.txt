#type: hscript

= opmenu =

"""Shows the items of a menu parameter on an operator."""

:usage: `opmenu [-l] [-a] <<op_name>> <<parm_name>> `

@options
    `-l`:
        Show name and label for each menu item.
    `-a`:
        Show all menu items, including separators.

@related
    - [opparm|Cmd:opparm]
    - [opscript|Cmd:opscript]
