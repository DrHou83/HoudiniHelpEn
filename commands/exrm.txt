#type: hscript

= exrm =

"""Removes custom expressions."""

:usage: `exrm [-v] <<pattern>> `

@options
    `-v`:
        Print verbose output, indicating which functions were removed.
    ` <<pattern>> `:
        Deletes any custom expression functions matching the pattern
