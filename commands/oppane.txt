#type: hscript

= oppane =

"""Show an editor for an operator."""

:usage: `oppane -t details|parmeditor <<operator_pattern>> `




@options
    `-t details|parmeditor`:
        The type of editor to show.

        details:
            Show the details view for a node. In SOPs, for example, this
            would be a geometry spreadsheet.
        parmeditor:
            Show the operator's parameter editor.
