#type: hscript

= mnew =

"""Resets the session."""

:usage: `mnew `

Current session will be replaced with an empty session. This is
equivalent to "File -> New" in an interactive session.



@examples
    {{{
    #!hscript
    mnew
    }}}
    Clear the current session.
