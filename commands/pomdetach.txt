#type: hscript

= pomdetach =

"""Detaches an operator from a persistent handle, or a handle from a
group."""

:usage: `pomdetach <<manip_name>> <<op_name>> `
:usage: `pomdetach -g <<manip_group_name>> <<manip_name>> `

@options
    `-g`:
        Detach a handle from a group.

@related
    - [pomadd|Cmd:pomadd]
    - [pomattach|Cmd:pomattach]
    - [pomclear|Cmd:pomclear]
    - [pomls|Cmd:pomls]
