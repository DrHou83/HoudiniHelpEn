An example of Sensor Panorama vop functions, it shows how to create a panorama view, specify the fisible objects through the use of bundles, and using cone lookup functions to identify the vision of an individual particle.

In this example, the agents are instructed to move towards the red sphere when it is visible to them. However when vision of the sphere is obscured by blockers or other agents. When the sphere is not visible the agent is instructed to move towards any visible agents.

Furthermore, the agents, blocker, and sphere are all represented by RBD objects.
