#helpid: h.pane.shaderview

= Shader view =

The shader view pane allows you to view the selected shader ball. You can rotate the ball by clicking ((LMB)) and dragging in the pane.

View menu:

    Sphere/Tube/Grid/Torus:
        Allows you to change the shape of the shader ball. The default shape is a sphere.
        
    Low/Medium/High Resolution:
        Allows you to change the resolution of the shader ball. The default resolution is medium.
    
    Home:
        Reverts the rotation of the shader ball back to its original position.

Background menu:
    Allows you to change the background of the pane. This is useful to use with shaders that have transparency or reflections.
