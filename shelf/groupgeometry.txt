= Group Geometry =

"""Generates groups of points, primitives, or edges according to various criteria."""

This tool allows you to group points, primitives, or edges by selecting them and simply clicking the Group Geometry tool on the shelf.

== Using Group Geometry == (includeme)

    # [Select|/basics/select] the points, primitives, edges, or vertices you want to group.
    
    # Click the [Icon:SOP/group] [Group Geometry|Node:sop/group] tool on the __Model__ tab of the shelf.
    
    # Enter a __Group Name__ in the operation controls toolbar or the parameter editor.
    
    [Image:/images/shelf/groupgeometry.png]
