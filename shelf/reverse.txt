= Reverse =

"""Reverses or cycles the vertex order of faces."""

This tool is often used to clean up polygons and NURBS by reversing their vertex order.

== Using Reverse == (includeme)
    
    # Select the faces or hulls to reverse and press ((Enter)) to confirm your selection.
        
    # Click the [Icon:SOP/reverse][Reverse|Node:sop/reverse] tool on the __Model__ tab.
    
    You can choose the vertex in the __Vertex__ drop-down menu in the [parameter editor|/ref/panes/parms].
    
    [Image:/images/shelf/reverse.png]

For specific parameter help see the [Icon:SOP/reverse][Reverse|Node:sop/reverse] node help.
