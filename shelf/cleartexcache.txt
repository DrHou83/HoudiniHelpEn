= Clear Texture Cache =

"""Clears the texture cache, forcing textures to be reloaded."""

Click the [Icon:SHELF/clear_texture_cache] Clear Texture Cache icon on the __Texture__ tab of the shelf to force a reload of any texture maps that have changed on disk.

- [Cmd:texcache]
