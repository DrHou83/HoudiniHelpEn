= Fan Force =

"""Applies forces on the objects as if a cone-shaped fan were acting on them."""

This tool can be used to simulate a hair dryer, or even blow popcorn as it is poured out of a bag.

== Using Fan Force == (includeme)
    
    # Select the dynamic object to apply fan force to.

    # Click the [Icon:DOP/fan][Fan Force|Node:dop/fan] tool on the __Drive Simulation__ tab.
