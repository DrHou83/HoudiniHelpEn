= Ray =

"""Projects one surface onto another."""

This tool is often used to drape [clothes|/shelf/clothobject] over surfaces, shrink-wrap one object with another, and other similar effects.

== Using Ray == (includeme)
    
    # Click the [Icon:SOP/ray][Ray|Node:sop/ray] tool on the __Model__ tab.
    
    # Select the points or primitives to project and press ((Enter)) to confirm your selection.
    
    # Select the primitives to project on and press ((Enter)) to confirm your selection.
    
    NOTE:
        This tool works best when smaller surfaces are projected onto larger surfaces.
    
    [Image:/images/shelf/ray.png]

For specific parameter help see the [Icon:SOP/ray][Ray|Node:sop/ray] node help.
