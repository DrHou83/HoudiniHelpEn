= Cut Hair =

"""Lets you interactively trim hair."""

:vimeo:Grooming hair 1
    #id: 116178807

This tool allows you to interactively remove hair from the scene. It is useful for trimming and styling hair. This tool will remove anything inside the brush radius.

NOTE:
    If you accidentally remove too much hair, you can easily use the [Extend Hair|/shelf/extendhair] tool to add more.

== Using Cut Hair == (includeme)

# Click the [Icon:FUR/cut_hair] [Cut Hair|Node:obj/guidegroom] tool on the __Grooming__ tab.

# Hold ((LMB)) and drag across the hair where you want to cut it.

TIP:
    Scrolling the mouse wheel or holding ((Shift)) while dragging ((LMB)) will let you resize your brush.

For specific parameter information, see the [Guide Groom|Node:obj/guidegroom] help.
