= Refine =

"""Increases the number of points/CVs in a curve or surface without changing its shape."""

This tool is useful for adding detail to [curves|/shelf/curve] and surfaces.

== Using Refine == (includeme)
    
    # Select the [curves|/shelf/curve] or surface to refine and press ((Enter)) to confirm your selection.

    # Click the [Icon:SOP/refine][Refine|Node:sop/refine] tool on the __Model__ tab.
    
    You can specify the amount of refinement in the [parameter editor|/ref/panes/parms].
    
    [Image:/images/shelf/refine.png]
    
For specific parameter help see the [Icon:SOP/refine][Refine|Node:sop/refine] node help.
