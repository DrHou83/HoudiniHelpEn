"""Returns the product of a list of numbers."""

:usage: `float product(float n)`
:usage: `int product(int n)`

    Returns the argument.

:usage: `float product(vector2 v)`
:usage: `float product(vector v)`
:usage: `float product(vector4 v)`

    Returns the product of the vector's components.

:usage: `int product(int arr[])`
:usage: `float product(float arr[])`

    Returns the product of the items of the array.

:usage: `<vector> product(<vector> arr[])`

    Returns a vector where the components are the products of the corresponding components in the vectors in the array.
