"""Returns the hyperbolic cosine of the argument."""

:usage: `float cosh(float n)`

    Returns the hyperbolic cosine of the argument.

:usage: `vector2 cosh(vector2 v)`
:usage: `vector cosh(vector v)`
:usage: `vector4 cosh(vector4 v)`

    Returns a new vector with `cosh()` applied to each component.
