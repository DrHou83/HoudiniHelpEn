"""Returns the inverse sine of the argument."""

:usage: `float asin(float n)`
    The inverse sine of `n`, where `n` is in radians. The return value is in the range -&pi;/2 to &pi;/2.

:usage: `vector2 asin(vector2 n)`
:usage: `vector asin(vector n)`
:usage: `vector4 asin(vector4 n)`

     Returns a new vector with `asin()` applied to all components.
