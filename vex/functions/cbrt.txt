"""Returns the cube root of the argument."""

:usage: `float cbrt(float n)`

    Returns the cube-root of `n`.

:usage: `vector2 cbrt(vector2 v)`
:usage: `vector cbrt(vector v )`
:usage: `vector4 cbrt(vector4 v )`

    Returns a new vector where each component is the cube-root of the corresponding component in `v`.
