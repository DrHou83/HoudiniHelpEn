= cyclet =

"""Channel segment function: repeats animation from previous frames."""

:usage: `cyclet(<<t1>>, <<t2>>)`

Repeats the animation from <<t1>> seconds to <<t2>> seconds.

If you wish to repeat the animation cumulatively, use
the [Exp:cycleoffsett] function instead.

@related

- [Exp:cycle]
- [Exp:cycleoffset]
- [Exp:cycleoffsett]
