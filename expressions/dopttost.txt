= dopttost =

"""Returns the simulation time equivalent to a given global time."""

:usage: `dopttost(<<dop>>, <<globaltime>>)`

This is similar to the [Exp:doptime] expression except
for the ability to specify a global time other than the current time.

@related
    - [Exp:dopsttot]
    - [Exp:doptime]
