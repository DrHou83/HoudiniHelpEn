= pointgrouplist =

"""Returns the list of point groups in a surface node."""

:usage: `pointgrouplist(<<surface_node>>)`

The returned string is a space separated list of group names.

To get a list of groups whose names match a pattern, use
[Exp:pointgroupmask].
