"""A shortcut for hou.pwd().hdaModule()."""

:usage: `phm()` -> [Hom:hou.HDAModule]

This shortcut lets you write `hou.phm()` instead of `hou.pwd().hdaModule()`.
You are most likely to use this shortcut from event handlers, button callbacks,
and menu generation scripts in digital assets.

See [Hom:hou.pwd] and [Hom:hou.Node#hdaModule] for more information.
